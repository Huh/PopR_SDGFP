Naming conventions for survival models (Needs updating)

age.month.year.covariates.txt

-  form of the model ms for multi-state and kf for known fate

-  age takes a value of s for a single age

-  sex takes a value of s for a single sex, a for all (no effect of sex) or fe for fixed effect of sex

-  month takes r for random effect, mvr for multivariate random

-  year takes s for a single year model

-  unit takes r for random effect, a for all and s for single or a for all (no effect of unit)

-  covariates take n to represent the absence of covariates
